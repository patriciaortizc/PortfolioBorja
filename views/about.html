ABOUT

I’m an FX Artist based in Madrid, with experience in film, episodic and independent productions.

I have worked with different studios and projects, both in-house and freelance. I also support FXDailies Studio as a Teaching Assistant, helping students with their shots, solving technical problems, and supporting dailies sessions.

I have been involved in different types of projects, from cinematic shorts to series work, including titles like Alienoid: Return to the Future, Parasyte: The Grey, and Take the Money and Run.

My work focuses on creating simulations that work well in production, keeping setups clean, and solving problems in an efficient way.
